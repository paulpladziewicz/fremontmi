Branstrom Park

By: Fremont Parks Department

Branstrom Park is the largest park in Fremont, offering over 100 acres of woodlands and various amenities such as trails, a lodge, and a playground.

Fremont Lake Park

Fremont Lake Park is a 17-acre park featuring a bathhouse, playground equipment, sand volleyball court, shelters, and more. It also has a boat launch and camping facilities.

Arboretum Park

This seven-acre park is devoted to native Michigan trees, shrubs, and wildflowers, making it an ideal spot for nature enthusiasts and families.

Veterans Memorial Park

Located in the heart of downtown, Veterans Memorial Park is designed for various events, festivals, and activities, making it a central gathering space for the community.

Fremont Dog Park

A secure off-leash park where well-behaved dogs can play and exercise, providing a safe environment for pets and their owners.
