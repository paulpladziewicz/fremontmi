Veterans Memorial Park

Address: 119 W Main St, Fremont, Michigan 49412

Located in the heart of downtown, Veterans Memorial Park is designed for various events, festivals, and activities, making it a central gathering space for the community.

Branstrom Park

Address: Branstrom Dr, Fremont, MI 49412

Branstrom Park is the largest park in Fremont, offering over 100 acres of woodlands and various amenities such as trails, a lodge, and a playground.

Fremont Lake Park

Address: 955 Cottage Grove, Fremont, MI 49412

Fremont Lake Park is a 17-acre park featuring a bathhouse, playground equipment, sand volleyball court, shelters, and more. It also has a boat launch and camping facilities.

Arboretum Park

Address: 529 Iroquois Dr, Fremont, MI 49412

This seven-acre park is devoted to native Michigan trees, shrubs, and wildflowers, making it an ideal spot for nature enthusiasts and families.

Fremont Dog Park

Address: 320 Lake Dr, Fremont, MI 49412

A secure off-leash park where well-behaved dogs can play and exercise, providing a safe environment for pets and their owners.

Fremont Christian Park

Address: 110 N Decker Ave, Fremont, MI, 49412

The park features multiple swings, a rock climbing structure that leads to exciting slides, and plenty of open space for kids to run and play freely. It’s a perfect spot for families looking for a fun and safe environment for their children to enjoy outdoor activities.

Church of the Living Christ Park

Address: 605 Hemlock St, Fremont, MI 49412

Designed with younger children in mind, it features a unique sand-covered terrain that’s perfect for imaginative play. The park is filled with various toys and play structures, providing endless entertainment for kids to explore, dig, and create.
