Tagging Guidelines: How to Make Your Content Easy to Find

Why Are Tags Important?

Tags play a crucial role in helping others discover your content on our platform. They act as labels that categorize your posts, businesses, events, or services, making it easier for the Fremont, MI community to find what they're looking for. The more accurate and relevant your tags, the more likely your content will be found by those interested.

By tagging correctly, you not only make your content stand out but also contribute to building a well-organized platform where people can easily connect and discover what's happening in their local area.

How Our Tagging System Works

  • Our platform uses tags to categorize content, making it flexible and adaptable. As more people use certain tags, those tags can eventually become broader categories or subcategories.
  • Popular tags can influence the structure of the platform, so your input directly shapes how others discover content.

Best Practices for Tagging

To make the most of the tagging system, follow these guidelines:

  • Choose Relevant Tags: Always pick tags that accurately describe your content. Think about what others might search for when looking for content like yours.
  • Keep It Simple and Concise: Avoid overly complicated or long tags. Stick to commonly understood terms that people are likely to use.
  • Use Proper Formatting:
    • Capitalize each word: e.g., "Language Learning."
    • Hyphens are allowed for compound words: e.g., "Self-Improvement."
    • Acronyms can be used but must be in uppercase (e.g., "DIY").

Example Tags

Here are some examples of well-formatted tags:

  • Cleaning
  • Lawn Care
  • Language Learning
  • ACT Prep
  • Self-Improvement

How to Add Tags

  1. Type Your Tag: In the "Tags" input field, type a tag that represents your content.
  2. Add It: Click "Add" or press "Enter" to confirm your tag. You can add up to 3 tags per entry.
  3. Manage Your Tags: Added tags will appear as labeled badges. You can remove any tag by clicking the “x” next to it if you want to make changes.

Important Notes:

  • You can use spaces within your tags to create multi-word tags (e.g., "Ice Cream").
  • Tags are limited to a maximum of 5 per entry to maintain focus and relevance.
  • Always check the "Tagging Guidelines" link next to the input field if you need a refresher.

How Tagging Benefits You

Proper tagging ensures that your content is more discoverable and organized. Here's how it benefits you:

  • Increased Visibility: Properly tagged content is more likely to be discovered by others in the community.
  • Better Categorization: Helps your content appear in relevant searches, making it easier for people to find exactly what they’re looking for.
  • Improved Platform Organization: Your tags help shape the platform’s categories, making the entire community experience smoother and more intuitive.
