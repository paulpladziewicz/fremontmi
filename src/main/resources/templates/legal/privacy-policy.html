Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains:

  • What information we collect
  • How we collect it (e.g., directly from users, through cookies)
  • How we use and share that information
  • How users can access and control their information
  • Your data retention practices
  • How we protect users' data
  • Contact information for privacy concerns

Information Collection

We collect various types of information in connection with the services we provide, including:

  • Personal Information: Information that identifies you as an individual, such as your name, email address, phone number, and other contact details. This information is typically collected when you create an account, join a group, post an event, or interact with other features of our platform.
  • Non-Personal Information: Information that does not directly identify you, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and the pages you visit on our platform. This information is collected through cookies, log files, and similar technologies to help us understand how our platform is being used.
  • Activity Information: Information about your interactions with the platform, such as group memberships, events you create or join, and other actions taken on the platform.
  • Payment Information: If you make transactions through our platform, such as paying for premium features or advertising, we may collect payment information, including credit card details or other payment methods. This information is processed securely through our payment processor and is not stored on our servers.

How We Collect Information

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Directly from You: When you create an account, post content, or interact with other users, you provide us with personal information directly.
  • Automatically: We use cookies, log files, and similar technologies to automatically collect non-personal information about your interactions with our platform.
  • From Third Parties: We may receive information from third-party services that you use to connect to our platform, such as social media logins or payment processors.

How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Providing Services: To operate, maintain, and improve our platform, including managing your account, facilitating group memberships, and allowing you to create or join events.
  • Communication: To communicate with you about your account, respond to inquiries, provide customer support, and send updates or notifications related to your activities on the platform.
  • Personalization: To customize your experience on the platform, such as showing you relevant groups, events, or other content based on your interests.
  • Security: To protect the security of our platform and its users, including preventing fraudulent activities and enforcing our terms of service.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations, such as responding to subpoenas or other legal requests.

How We Share Your Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Information you post publicly, such as in groups or event pages, will be visible to other users of the platform.
  • With Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating the platform, such as hosting providers, payment processors, or email service providers. These service providers are required to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to a legal request, such as a court order or subpoena.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

Cookies and Session Storage

We use cookies and session storage for the purpose of authenticating users and maintaining session information. These cookies are essential for the operation of our platform, allowing users to stay logged in and access their accounts securely. These cookies do not collect personal data for advertising purposes and are not used to track your browsing activity.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide the services you have requested, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When your information is no longer required, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and Update: You can access and update your personal information through your account settings.
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal information, subject to any legal obligations we may have to retain certain information.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of receiving marketing communications from us by following the unsubscribe instructions in those messages. Please note that you may still receive administrative or service-related communications.
  • Data Portability: You can request a copy of your personal information in a machine-readable format.

How We Protect Your Data

We take the security of your personal information seriously. We implement a variety of security measures to protect your data, including encryption, access controls, and secure servers. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
