List Your Business

Get your business listed on the platform to be easily found by new and returning customers. Plus, each business listing comes with a built-in contact form so customers can reach out to you directly, without friction.

01
Overview & Pricing

Select a subscription plan for your business listing, either monthly or annually. Each subscription applies to a single business listing.

02
Create Your Business Listing

Fill in your core business details with a simple form. This information will help customers understand what your business provides and find you easily.

03
Complete Your Subscription

Enter your payment details to activate your subscription. Once your subscription is active, your business will be listed on the platform and ready to be discovered. Customers can reach out to you directly through a built-in contact form, with inquiries sent straight to your inbox.
